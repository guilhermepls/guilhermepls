<h2 align="left">/ Learn a bit about me</h2>

###

<p align="left">I'm Guilherme Pinheiro, a <strong>Cross-Platform Developer</strong> (specialising in <strong>Full-Stack Development</strong>) currently studying for my 5th semester of my bachelor's degree in <strong>Software Engineering</strong> at the Federal University of Ceará (UFC).

I enjoy contributing to open source projects and expanding my knowledge. In 2022, I entered university and began my studies in programming. At university, I learned about the importance of designing systems that are intuitive and accessible to the user, as well as how software processes and their modelling contribute to quality and efficiency in project development.

During the Database Fundamentals course, I developed a database using <strong>PostgreSQL</strong> and implemented a <strong>CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete)</strong> in <strong>Jupyter</strong>, using the <strong>Python</strong> language to interact with the database. In 2024, in the Web Development course, I created a <strong>REST API</strong> with <Strong>Node.js</strong>, <strong>Express</strong>, <strong>MongoDB</strong> and <strong>Mongoose</strong>. The application implemented a complete <strong>CRUD</strong> with <strong>GET</strong> operations to list and search for resources by ID, <strong>POST</strong> to create new resources, <strong>PUT</strong> to update existing resources by ID and <strong>DELETE</strong> to remove resources by ID. I used <strong>Postman</strong> to test and validate the functioning of the API.

Outside of programming, I'm passionate about sports, especially football. I also have a keen interest in music; I started playing the guitar in 2020 and it's been a very rewarding experience. In addition, I'm interested in psychology and self-development, and I enjoy going to the gym and travelling to get to know new cultures and explore new places.</p>
